Farming Tips & Best Practices

  • Test your soil before planting to know the right fertilizer and crops for your land.
  • Use certified seeds for better yield and disease resistance.
  • Practice crop rotation to maintain soil fertility and reduce pests.
  • Weed your farm regularly, especially in the early stages of crop growth.
  • Apply fertilizer at the recommended times and rates for each crop.
  • Harvest crops at the right maturity for best quality and market value.
  • Store harvested crops in a cool, dry place to prevent spoilage.
  • Join a local farmers' group or cooperative for support and shared knowledge.

Did You Know?

  • Early planting at the start of the rainy season helps crops establish strong roots.
  • Mulching helps retain soil moisture and suppress weeds.
  • Integrated pest management (IPM) reduces the need for chemical pesticides.
